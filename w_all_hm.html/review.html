REVIEW

Steven perez August 14, 2023

Chicago P.d for me is a great tv series because I really like the back story about each character
inside of the tv series. The fact that every one get affected by the case they work on. Sometimes
the case affected all them. I do love the fact that the character actually died during pursue or
during shootout.

I don't have a favorite character. I do love how every character act difrent on every circumstance
the development that everyone has on the series for me is great.

    Trivia

  • Fact #1
    • Jason Beghe appeared as Hank Voight in 5 of Dick Wolf's shows He is Dick Wolf's favorite since his first appearance as Hank Voight in Chicago Fire. So dick decided to also put him in 5 of his other shows: Chicago Fire, Chicago PD, Chicago Med, Chicago Justice and Law and Order: Special Victims Unit. Essential!

  • Fact #2
    • In Chicago PD, actors often forget one essential detail as cops In the USA, when police officers make an arrest, they have to read them their rights, but in the show this aspect is pretty often forgotten.
